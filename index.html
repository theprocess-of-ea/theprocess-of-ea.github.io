Pollock and the Process

Andrea Vorametsanti and Emma Ishida
PSY09 Creative Project

Who is Pollock? Pollock's Works We made Art!

Abstract art is a highly subjective style of art. Some people pay millions of dollars to hang a piece in their home while others think of it as something "anyone can do". Before starting this project, we were more like the latter group, but we wanted to investigate this topic because of its ties to the subconscious. Upon more research, both of us realized that perhaps abstract art is not just a random collection of colors and shape, but a product of intentionality. Ultimately, we came to research what creativity is, which is the generation of new ideas. However, some people come better equipped with the ability to generate new ideas based on brain structure and ability, creating the innovation that we see as creative. With this in mind, our project aims to investigate Pollock's way of creating art while also learning about abstract art as a whole.
